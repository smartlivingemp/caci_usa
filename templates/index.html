Our Beliefs

Guided by a divine purpose, we are rooted in Vision, Mission, Core Values, and Tenets of Faith.

Our Vision

Knowing Christ and making disciples of all nations, tribes, and peoples constituting them into Bible-believing Church for the worship, edification, and evangelization of the world.

Our Mission

We are on a mission to serve and do the work of God, bringing joy and salvation to all people and bringing them closer to God.

Our Core Values

The unity of the Godhead and the Trinity of the persons therein. The utter depravity of human nature, the necessity for Repentance and Regeneration, and the Eternal Doom of the finally impenitent.

Church Tenets

The Church

The Christ Apostolic Church International (CAC), a non-governmental organisation, was founded by Apostle Peter Anim. He and his Faith Tabernacle Church (which later became known as the Christ Apostolic Church) are credited with the origins of Classical Pentecostalism in Ghana. An interest in a Christian periodical, "The Sword of the Spirit", led him to fraternize, through correspondence, with its editor, Pastor A. Clark, founder of the Faith Tabernacle Church, Philadelphia.
