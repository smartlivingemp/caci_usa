Our Ministries

Discover the vibrant arms of service within CACI-USA

Evangelism Ministry

“Go therefore and make disciples of all nations...” — Matthew 28:19 Bringing the message of Christ to the world, one soul at a time. Our Evangelism Ministry is dedicated to outreach, sharing the gospel, and igniting faith in every heart.

Men's Ministry

“Be watchful, stand firm in the faith, act like men, be strong.” — 1 Corinthians 16:13 Equipping men to lead, serve, and grow as spiritual anchors in their homes and communities. This ministry inspires godly manhood through mentorship, fellowship, and study.

Women's Ministry

“She is clothed with strength and dignity...” — Proverbs 31:25 Empowering women to grow spiritually, support one another, and lead with grace. This ministry nurtures the heart of every woman through fellowship, service, and prayer.

Youth Ministry

“Don’t let anyone look down on you because you are young, but set an example...” — 1 Timothy 4:12 Igniting purpose, passion, and identity in Christ for the next generation. Our Youth Ministry empowers young believers to grow in faith, lead with boldness, and shine in a challenging world.

Children's Ministry

“Let the little children come to me...” — Matthew 19:14 Planting seeds of faith in young hearts through teaching, fun, and care. We shape tomorrow’s leaders by grounding them in the love and truth of God’s Word.
